<p><strong>The Handheld 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structure Light Scanner</li><li>Laser Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld 3D scanner market is primarily categorized into Structure Light Scanners and Laser Scanners. Structure Light Scanners project a series of light patterns onto an object, capturing its shape via deviations in the projected light, making them ideal for detailed surface capture in controlled environments. Laser Scanners, on the other hand, utilize laser beams to measure distances and create precise 3D models, excelling in outdoor or larger-scale applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, architecture, and healthcare.</p></p>
